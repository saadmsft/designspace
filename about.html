THE STUDIO

+ A QUIET PRACTICE
01 — STORY

Founded in 2012, Space Studio is an independent multidisciplinary design practice operating from New York and Milan.

We are a small, intentionally non-scaling team of architects, designers and craftspeople. Our work is shaped by a deep interest in monastic architecture, Japanese spatial philosophy, Shaker craft and mid-century European modernism.

What we build is not about novelty. It is about permanence — objects and spaces that will still make sense in fifty years.

APPROACH

+ HOW WE WORK
02 — METHOD

We believe good design is a form of subtraction — the disciplined removal of everything that is not essential.

We accept a limited number of commissions each year. Every project is led personally by one of our three partners, supported by a cross-disciplinary team and a trusted network of collaborators.

We move between scales without changing our voice: a chair is designed with the same rigour as a building, a doorhandle with the same care as a masterplan.
