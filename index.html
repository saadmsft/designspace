+ ABOUT THE STUDIO
01 — STATEMENT

At Space Studio, we design the full spectrum of the built environment — from the monolithic silhouette of a building, to the curvature of a chair, to the weight of an object in your hand.

Our work is defined by restraint, proportion and an obsession with material honesty. We believe good design is a form of subtraction — the disciplined removal of everything that is not essential.

A multidisciplinary practice operating from New York and Milan, we accept a limited number of commissions each year. Every project is led personally by one of our three partners.
