02 — SERVICES

Four interconnected disciplines, one design language. Whether a single chair or an entire building, each commission is approached with the same conceptual rigour and material sensitivity.

We work globally across residential, cultural, commercial and consumer sectors — in close collaboration with engineers, craftspeople and manufacturers.

+01 / ARCHITECTURE

ARCHITECTURE
DESIGN

From private residences to cultural landmarks, our architecture practice explores the dialogue between mass, void, and light. Every project begins with the site — its history, its climate, its silences — and evolves through an obsession with proportion and material honesty.

We collaborate closely with engineers, contractors and craftspeople to deliver buildings that age gracefully and reward slow inhabitation.

  • SCOPERESIDENTIAL · CULTURAL · COMMERCIAL
  • STAGESCONCEPT → CONSTRUCTION
  • TIMELINE12 – 36 MONTHS
  • STARTING FEEFROM $85,000
+02 / INTERIORS

INTERIOR
DESIGN

Interiors that feel inevitable. We compose each room as a sequence of tactile moments — the cool of polished stone, the warmth of linen, the hush of a shadow falling across a wall.

From turnkey residences to boutique hospitality, we specify every surface, fixture and object with the same level of care.

  • SCOPEHOMES · HOTELS · WORKSPACES
  • STAGESCONCEPT → STYLING
  • TIMELINE6 – 18 MONTHS
  • STARTING FEEFROM $40,000
+03 / FURNITURE

FURNITURE
DESIGN

Our furniture practice sits at the intersection of sculpture and function. Each piece is developed in-studio with a small network of master craftspeople in Italy, Denmark and Japan.

We offer bespoke commissions, limited editions, and licensed collections for galleries and manufacturers.

  • SCOPEBESPOKE · EDITION · PRODUCTION
  • MATERIALSSOLID WOOD · STONE · BLACKENED STEEL
  • TIMELINE4 – 10 MONTHS
  • STARTING FEEFROM $12,000
+04 / PRODUCTS

PRODUCT
DESIGN

We reduce objects to their essence — eliminating ornament until only function, form and feeling remain. Our product work spans lighting, tableware, consumer electronics and packaging.

From initial sketch to tooling, prototyping, and brand rollout, we guide products through every phase of their life.

  • SCOPELIGHTING · HOMEWARE · ELECTRONICS
  • STAGESRESEARCH → LAUNCH
  • TIMELINE3 – 12 MONTHS
  • STARTING FEEFROM $18,000
